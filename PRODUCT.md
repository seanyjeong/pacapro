## Product Purpose

P-ACA is the operating console for a physical-education entrance academy: students, 상담, schedules, attendance, instructors, payments, reports, and notifications. Success means the academy can run the day from one reliable interface with fewer missed tasks, fewer duplicated actions, and faster handoffs between PC, tablet, and mobile surfaces.

## Brand Personality

Precise, composed, field-ready. The interface should feel like a premium operations desk, not a generic admin template or a playful SaaS landing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid dated admin templates, heavy card grids, large rounded shadow boxes, decorative gradients, emoji buttons, and low-density dashboard decoration. Do not make the product feel like a marketing page, a finance dashboard clone, or a dark-blue generic SaaS console.

## Design Principles

1. Put the current operating day first: 상담, 수업, 결제, and urgent follow-ups should be visible before decorative metrics.
2. Preserve density, but create clear hierarchy so important work does not compete with every table cell.
3. Use one consistent command vocabulary across PC, tablet, and mobile.
4. Keep product confidence high: restrained color, precise spacing, and obvious states.
5. Separate exploration from production: redesign ideas are proven in the lab before back-porting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, non-color-only status communication, reduced-motion safe transitions, and Korean plain-language empty/error states.
